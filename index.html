About Solar Energy

What is DNI?

Direct Normal Irradiance (DNI) measures how much solar energy is received per unit area at a given location. It's the most important metric for concentrated solar power systems.

Solar Energy Benefits

  • Renewable and sustainable energy source
  • Reduces electricity bills
  • Low maintenance costs
  • Environmentally friendly

Ideal Solar Conditions

Areas with DNI values above 5 kWh/m²/day are considered excellent for solar energy production. Values between 4-5 kWh/m²/day are good, while below 3 kWh/m²/day may require alternative solutions.
